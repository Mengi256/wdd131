Uganda is a landlocked country in East Africa, known for its natural beauty, diverse wildlife, and rich culture. It is often called "The Pearl of Africa" due to its stunning landscapes, including lakes, mountains, and national parks. The capital city is Kampala. Uganda is home to many ethnic groups, and its official languages are English and Swahili. The economy mainly depends on agriculture, but tourism is also growing. Despite past challenges, Uganda continues to develop and grow steadily.
